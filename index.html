Timeline

Milestones in mina's build journey.

  1. 2024 · September

    Project kickoff

    The SAPIEN-AIT team forms at EPFL and the mina project begins.

  2. 2024 · November

    First mechanical concept

    Initial CAD of the leg and joint architecture comes together.

  3. 2025 · February

    First electronics bring-up

    Custom PCBs power on and talk to the first actuators on the bench.

  4. 2025 · May

    First leg assembled

    A full leg is built and the control software drives its first motion.

  5. 2025 · October

    Full body integration

    All limbs are mounted on the torso and the full robot stands for the first time.

  6. 2026 · March

    First standing demo

    mina holds a stable standing posture under closed-loop control.
